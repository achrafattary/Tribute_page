The fascinating life of Nikola Tesla

the father of alternating current

Here's some informations about Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - Tesla was born in Smiljan in the Austo-Hungarian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
  • 1873 - He attended the Realschule, Karlstadt
  • 1881 - He took a job as an electrical engineer at a telephone company in Budapest
  • 1883 - he built a prototype of the induction motor
  • 1882 - Tesla discovered the rotating magnetic field
  • 1888 - Tesla wrote a classic paper called "A New System of Alternating Current Motors and Transformers"
  • 1891 - Tesla developed an induction coil that produced high-frequency alternating currents
  • 1896 - Tesla patented the basic system of radio
  • 1943 - Tesla died
